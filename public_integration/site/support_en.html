FAQ

The preview button did not appear. Refresh the lead/contact card page. The button appears next to files in the notes feed after the integration is installed.

"File is too large". Preview limits: 15 MB for Office formats, 50 MB for legacy formats (.doc/.xls/.ppt), 2 MB for Markdown. Download larger files instead.

"Too many requests". Rate limiting kicked in. Wait a minute and retry.

Where do my files go? See the privacy policy: most formats are processed right in your browser; docx/pptx/xlsx/csv are rendered via Microsoft Office Viewer.

Report an issue

Email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} with: your account address, the file format, and the error text from the modal window. We never need your file contents.
