**Nexus Looker — preview files without downloading**

Tired of downloading every attachment just to peek inside? Nexus Looker adds an
eye button next to every file in the lead, contact and company timeline. One click —
and the document opens in a preview window right inside your CRM.

**What it handles:**
- PDF and images (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG) — instantly, right in the browser;
- Word, Excel, PowerPoint (.docx, .xlsx, .pptx) and CSV — via Microsoft Office Viewer;
- legacy formats (.doc, .xls, .ppt, .rtf, .odt) — converted to PDF automatically;
- plain text and Markdown — neatly formatted.

**Why your team will love it:**
- see the contents of an invoice, contract or deck in a second — no downloaded
  copies cluttering your computer;
- works in lead, contact and company cards;
- zero configuration — install and use.

**The integration is free.**

**Data transparency:** PDF, images and text are processed right in your browser and
never leave it. To display .docx/.pptx/.xlsx/.csv files, they are temporarily
(up to 5 minutes) passed to Microsoft Office Viewer. Details in the
<a href="https://nexus-oko.naithon.one/legal/privacy_en.html">privacy policy</a>.
